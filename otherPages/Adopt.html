Success Story

Simba - Street Survivor to Family Protector

Simba was a stray dog found on the streets of Kathmandu, weak and fearful. After being adopted by a loving family through Tail of Hopes, Simba slowly gained confidence and became a loyal protector of his family. Today, he enjoys long walks and provides unwavering love and protection.

Sita - From Abandoned Puppy to Happy Companion

Sita was abandoned and malnourished before being rescued by Tail of Hopes. After adoption, her new family gave her the care she needed, and she transformed into a playful, joyful dog. Sita now enjoys her life with her loving family, playing and snuggling by the fire.

Raju - Overcoming Fear and Finding Freedom

Raju, once abandoned and traumatized, was adopted by a kind family who patiently helped him heal. With time and love, Raju overcame his fears and became a playful, free-spirited dog. He now loves running around the yard and enjoys a happy, secure life.
